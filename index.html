Tropic Thunder

Movie Review

Absolte top tier movie. Hilarious, interesting, thrilling, pretty much perfect. The casting is amazing too, getting celebrities and actors everyone knows and can recognize, giving them silly parts with funny, and offensive characters. Only issue with it is that it isn't long enough. That being said, 9.5/10 movie.

John Wick

Movie Review
John Wick is just straight up awesome, sure it's a little unrealistic but it's so cool! The crazy weapons he pulls out of nowhere and finds laying around, and the insane ways he uses his environment and his weapons to take down bad guys is awesome. Also, Keanu Reeves' acting is amazing, the casting is perfect, and the story is super cool. Overall, 9.5/10.
